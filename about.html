Our Story

Umkhanyakude Farm is a growing poultry enterprise based in Ngwenya Village, Eswatini. Founded and managed by a passionate young entrepreneur, we deliver healthy, well-raised chickens to communities and businesses across the region.

Mission

To provide high-quality poultry while upholding animal welfare and sustainable farming practices.

Values

Quality, integrity, community, sustainability.

Farm Practices

Animal Welfare

Brooding, nutrition, and space standards to ensure healthy growth.

Biosecurity

Controlled access, hygiene protocols, and vaccination schedules.

Sustainability

Efficient feed use, waste management, and local sourcing.

Highlights

Reliable Supply

Day-old, 3-week, and market-ready broilers available year-round.

Community Support

Training and consultations for new poultry farmers.

Quality Assurance

Consistent growth and uniform weights through best practices.
