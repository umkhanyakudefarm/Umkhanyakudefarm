Care Guide

Brooding

Provide 32–34°C at start, clean water, and starter feed. Reduce temperature weekly.

Nutrition

Starter, grower, finisher feeds per recommended schedule. Ensure constant clean water.

Health

Maintain dry litter and ventilation. Follow vaccination recommendations.
