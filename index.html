UMKHANYAKUDE FARM

Umkhanyakude Farm is a growing poultry enterprise based in Ngwenya Village, Eswatini. Founded and managed by a young, passionate entrepreneurial woman, the farm is dedicated to delivering healthy, well-raised chickens to the community and beyond. With a strong commitment to quality and care, Umkhanyakude Farm specializes in brooding and rearing chicks from their earliest stages, ensuring they grow in a safe, healthy, and well-managed environment.

About Our Business

The business aims to meet the rising demand for quality poultry in Ngwenya by supplying local households, restaurants, and small businesses with strong, well-nurtured birds. Driven by ambition and vision, the owner intends to scale production over time and expand the farm's reach to new markets outside Ngwenya—providing reliable poultry solutions to a wider audience.

Our Vision

To become the leading poultry farm in Eswatini, recognized for quality, reliability, and sustainable farming practices that contribute to food security and economic growth.

Our Mission

To provide healthy, well-raised poultry products to our community while empowering local farmers through knowledge sharing and creating sustainable employment opportunities.

Our Goals

1. Increase production capacity by 50% within the next two years
2. Expand market reach to three new regions in Eswatini
3. Implement sustainable farming practices that minimize environmental impact
4. Create 10 new employment opportunities for local youth

Umkhanyakude Farm stands as a symbol of dedication, empowerment, and agricultural excellence, contributing to food security and economic growth within the community.

Chick Rearing & Brooding Services

At Umkhanyakude Farm, we specialize in the critical early stages of chick development. Our brooding services ensure chicks receive optimal warmth, nutrition, and care during their first weeks of life.

We offer:

  • Temperature-controlled brooding facilities
  • Specialized starter feeds for optimal growth
  • Vaccination and health monitoring
  • Expert care by trained poultry handlers
  • Consultation services for new poultry farmers

Our brooding process ensures chicks develop strong immune systems and healthy growth patterns, setting the foundation for successful meat production.

We also offer training workshops for local farmers interested in starting their own poultry operations.
